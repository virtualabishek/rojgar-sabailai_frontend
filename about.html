What we are doing

Connecting Workers and Job Hirers

Our project aims to connect semi-skilled workers from villages directly with job hirers.

By facilitating communication, addressing technology gaps, and emphasizing direct interaction, we empower individuals to secure meaningful employment opportunities.

How it works

1. Search a job

First, search for a job. If you believe you are capable of it, then apply. If you don’t have proper access to technology, inquire with your municipality to see if they can assist.

2. Apply for job

If you are interested in working, apply for the job. If you don’t have the necessary technology to apply, ask your municipality for help or assistance.

3. Get your job

If you feel ready to work, talk directly with the company When you feel prepared, Discuss pricing, responsibilities, and next steps.
